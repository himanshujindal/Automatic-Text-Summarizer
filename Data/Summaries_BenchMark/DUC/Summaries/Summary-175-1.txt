Chronology of the Exxon Valdez oil spill: March 24, the tanker runs
aground; March 30, Exxon fires Captain Joseph Hazelwood after
determining he was drunk; September 15, Exxon suspends beach clean up
for the winter claiming a job well done but Alaskan officials
disagree; January 22, Hazelwood goes on trial in Anchorage; February
27, a federal grand jury indicts Exxon and its subsidiary on five
criminal counts; March 22, the jury finds Hazelwood guilty of
negligent discharge of oil but innocent of more serious charges.
