The official opening of the Channel tunnel on May 6 should be the
starting point for recovery in the property market in Kent and
Northeastern France.  Far-sighted prospective buyers looking near
Calais include hauliers wanting to set up a base on the Continent and
Asian entrepreneurs who see an advantage in being close to Belgium.

Growing confidence in the UK market is slowly stimulating interest in
French property, although smaller banks in Calais are none too happy
when Britons seek loans.  Show cash and there are bargains to be had.
Wine Society members can buy wine free from UK taxes.
