Police said members of Shining Path, a Maoist group, killed two policemen and wounded three in jungle raids. The Rodrigo Franco Command, which has vowed to kill a Shining Path member or sympathizer for every person slain by guerrillas, issued the threat against District Attorney Carlos Escobar on Monday, according to his office in Andean city of Ayacucho. The Rodrigo Franco group is named for an official of the government party killed the Shining Path killed last year.
